Hello!

I'm Jess! Hailing all the way from Vancouver, Canada and now living in Sydney... And yes, I do like everything with a drizzle of maple syrup. I remember going to my first website and thinking "Whoaaaaa!". It was a website for the movie Space Jam, which you can still see by the way! (Space Jam) Being a child of the internet age, I was always curious about how the internet worked. I was 12 years old when I got Nexopia, similar to Myspace but exclusively for Canadians. The interesting thing about Nexopia was that you could customize your profile page using HTML... This was in 2003! Fast forward 13 years, and I've found myself back in the same curious frame of mind. After 3 years of Autism research, I decided I needed a change in my life. One Saturday morning, I was looking up coding bootcamps in Sydney... And General Assembly popped up. The rest is history.

Today, whenever I see something, I want to know how it was built and using what. In General Assembly, I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, SQL, Ruby, Rails, Backbone.js, Node.js, React.js, Phaser.js, and D3. In the near future, I'd like to solidify my ability with React.js and move towards React Native. Whatever it may be, I'm hoping to use my skills and my curiosity to excel in web development and produce cool things!

FUN FACT: I learned how to use a bow and arrow after watching "The Hunger Games"
